## 👋 About me

I am a Ph.D. student at the Mohamed bin Zayed University of Artificial Intelligence (MBZUAI), currently a research intern with the Visual Computing Group at Microsoft Research Asia (MSRA).

My current research focuses on **agentic data**: data and executable environments that let AI agents act. My earlier work spans **data optimization**, including dataset distillation and dataset pruning, as well as **adversarial attacks** on large vision-language models.

## 🔬 Research interests

- **Agentic Data:** data synthesis and executable environments for computer-use and tool-using agents
- **Data-Centric AI:** dataset pruning, dataset distillation, and data-efficient training
- **Adversarial Robustness:** transferable black-box attacks on large vision-language models
